<p style = "margin-top: -2px;"> <span style="font-size: 25px;">&ensp;M</span>ysterious and always wearing her mask, Roshan is quiet and nervous, though she tries very hard to help everyone with her healing abilities. Growing up an orphan, her strange markings and even stranger dreams at night encouraged bullying from the other kids, even though all she tried to be was kind.</p>
<p style = "margin-top: -5px;"> &ensp;When her dreams began to prophesize a strange plague spreading in the land, Roshan fled into the woods in an attempt to begin researching a cure. The herbs she found and grew do seem to help the afflicted, however it doesn't last for long. Unsatisfied with her work so far, she followed another dream to Riverton, and is now swept up in an adventure much larger than herself.</p>
<p style = "margin-top: -5px;"> &ensp;She's committed to seeing this quest to the end; however, she's seen others with the same sickness, which worries her that it has begun to spread outside of her quiet little town.</p>
