<p style = "margin-top: -2px;"> <span style="font-size: 25px;">&ensp;S</span>eemingly always cheerful, Deliverance enjoys delivering each letter she receives almost as much as she enjoys the job itself. However, she'd be lying if the real reason for taking up the family tradition of the mail bag was anything else but spite. Betrayed by her own father, who attempted to pass on the role to her step-brother, she stole the bag for herself and has committed to carrying out his legacy for himself.</p>
<p style = "margin-top: -5px;"> &ensp;Even as she's always focused on delivering letters, she's found herself invited to join a party of fellow adventures as they serve a mysterious benefactor. Deliverance enjoys the company of each of her companions, considering them all her friends. Even as friends, she's still kept much of her past a secret from them.</p>
<p style = "margin-top: -5px;"> &ensp;Even as the party has delved into deeper family conspiracies and vaults far beyond anything they had imagined, she still has one primary goal; to finish her job.</p>
