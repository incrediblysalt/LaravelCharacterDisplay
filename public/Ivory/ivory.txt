<p style = "margin-top: -2px;"> <span style="font-size: 25px;">&ensp;A</span> keen shot with a sharp tongue, Ivory enjoys the life and thrills of adventuring. Growing up, she enjoyed the mystery of magic, though had no ability to use it herself. Instead, she encouraged her younger sister to grow the innate magic she was lucky to be born with.</p>
<p style = "margin-top: -5px;"> &ensp;Meanwhile, Ivory spent her time working with secretive organizations, establishing herself as a proficient information gatherer and even assassin. It wasn't until she branched off to find herself in adventures that her own magic started to grow, even without her noticing for a while.</p>
<p style = "margin-top: -5px;"> &ensp;Now, she spends her time defending the town of Aramoor with her friend Placidus and spending her free time exploring her magic. While it's not the same thrills of dungeons and prophesies she's seen before, she does enjoy this small town life with him.</p>
